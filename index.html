A Pioner e a Macc Marland partilham a visão “easy boating”. Assim queremos que os proprietários dos barcos Pioner tenham uma experiência única em possuir um barco que é seguro, durável e fácil de usar. Na Macc Marland - o distribuidor Pioner exclusivo para Portugal - pode escolher entre vários modelos de barcos em diversas áreas de uso tais como família, laser ativo, pedalo e profissional.

Pioner Multi

       Pioner MULTI

Easy Boating

      IT’S ALL ABOUT MASTERING THE MAJORITY OF CHALLENGES AT SEA

      It’s all in the name. The Pioner Multi has such an intelligent design
      that its usability stretches from that of an emergency boat to a fishing boat,
      to a diving boat and a country boat.

       Professional seafarers love this boat, which offers extensive features
       and a loading capacity of 1,350kg.

         The bow port which is now 92cm wide is lowered with a rust-free hoist,
       which eases embarking/disembarking and loading/unloading.

      It has a simple design and is extremely seaworthy.
     There are many more than just professional boaters who need a Pioner Multi.
